How NFL Analytics Affect Draft Priority
  • Consistency Score (0-100%): How reliable a player's performance is based on game-to-game variation
  • Upside Potential (0-200%): Future performance potential based on career trajectory and age
  • Risk Factor (0-100%): Injury and performance risk assessment
  • Trend Score (-100% to +100%): Recent performance trend analysis
  • NFL-Adjusted Priority: Final draft priority incorporating all NFL data using the formula: Base Points + (Consistency × 20) + (Upside × 15) - (Risk × 25) + (Trend × 10)
